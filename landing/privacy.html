Privacy Policy (v2.1)

Last updated: June 2026

1. Data Collection & Purpose

Astryxion API collects minimal personal data to provide our services under the legal bases of Contract Performance and Legitimate Interest:

  • Account Data: E-mail and Hashed Passwords for authentication.
  • Infrastructure Data: IP addresses and User-Agent for security audits and anti-fraud.
  • API Usage: Request counts and latency for billing and performance monitoring.

2. Data Subject Rights (LGPD & GDPR)

As a data subject, you have the right to request access to your data, its correction, portability, or deletion. To exercise these rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer (DPO).

Data Protection Officer (DPO): Astryxion Privacy Team
Contact: support@astryxion.com

3. Security & Retention

We use AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit. We store esoteric calculation parameters (birth data) for a maximum of 24 hours in our calculation cache to improve performance; after this period, the ephemeral data is permanently purged.
